Travelman

Game Overview

Travelman is an engaging trivia game where you identify countries based on their flags. Each round challenges you to guess the name of the country using only its flag as a clue.

Objective: You have 5 guesses total to correctly identify as many flags as possible. To win, successfully guess all the flags shown without exhausting your 5 guesses.

How to Play: To start, you must hit the "Play" button. Each round displays a flag on the right side of the screen. Type the full name of the country into the input field.
